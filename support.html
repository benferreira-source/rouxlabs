Frequently Asked

How does FlexPort mount to a rack?+

FlexPort snaps onto the vertical rail of any standard 19″ rack. No screws, no zip ties, no tools. Snap on, snap off.

What's the difference between Left and Right?+

Each FlexPort routes cables out one side of the rack. Left versions route to the left of the rack, Right versions to the right. Choose your side based on which way your patch cables run. For symmetrical installs, order one Left set and one Right set.

What cable types does it support?+

FlexPort fits standard Cat5e, Cat6, and Cat6A patch cables. The cable saddles maintain bend radius spec for Cat6A.

Why is it sold in sets?+

A full rack looks right when every unit matches. Sets of 2, 4, 8, and 12 let you size a single deployment cleanly. Need a custom set size? Email hello@rouxlabs.com.
