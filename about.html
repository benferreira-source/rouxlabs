Every rack has a grey space — the gap between a patch panel doing its job and the work actually looking the way it should. The loose loop behind the door. The zip-tied label that flipped backwards. The lacing bar that doesn't quite line up with the rail.

Roux Labs is an R&D company that also sells products. We design hardware first for the installer, then for the rack — patented, no-tool, made in the USA.

FlexPort is our first product. It won't be the last.
