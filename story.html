The Problem

Johnny spends his days inside other people's racks — recommending gear, supporting integrators, watching installs go in. The same gap kept showing up. A site needed one, two, maybe four ports near a switch or an appliance. The only honest answer was a full patch panel, eating a whole rack unit for ports that would never get used.

In a fully populated cabinet, that math gets uglier fast. "If a rack or cabinet is fully populated, the only option is to purchase a new enclosure." A $2,000 cabinet to add four jacks.

The Solution

FlexPort mounts directly to the rack rail using the screw that's already there. It uses standard keystone jacks. It doesn't consume a rack unit. It stacks. It works in open-air and closed-door cabinets. No special tools.

Field-tested with Datastrait Networks across MN, WI, IL, SD, and ND before it shipped. Patent secured in May 2026.
